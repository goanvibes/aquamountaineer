Our Custom Build Process.

We take the guesswork out of building large or complex aquatic setups.

1. The Vision & Dimensions

Send us a WhatsApp message with the physical dimensions of your space, your aesthetic preferences (e.g., heavily planted, minimalist rocks, specific fish), and your budget.

2. Equipment Matching

We calculate the exact water volume to recommend the appropriate filtration capacity (like canister filters), lighting spectrums, and substrate depth so your ecosystem won't crash.

3. The Hardscape Setup

Select from our imported Dragon Stone, Seiryu stone, and driftwood. We guide you on the 'rule of thirds' and structural stability for 3D backgrounds before you add water.
